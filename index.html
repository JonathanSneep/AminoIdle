Click here to play Amino Idle

Amino Idle is an incremental-idle browser game that crafts unique worlds on every playthrough. The game uses lots of fun geeky stuff like cellular automata to generate a unique world and flocking/path optimization/maze generation etc, this game is a window into a dynamically evolving universe. It's a labor of love, blending intricate mechanics with spontaneous creativity, and I'm excited to share it with the world. Please try it out!
